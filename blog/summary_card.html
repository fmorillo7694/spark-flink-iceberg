Flink holds ~17 GB flat, append or upsert. Both Spark upsert modes pin ~30–31 GB as their backlog piles up in the heap.

4. Where the time goes

~97% of every Spark micro-batch is the Iceberg write — the commit path is the wall. The levers that matter are write parallelism (bucket count) and merge strategy (merge-on-read ≫ copy-on-write), not raw compute.
For high-rate upsert into Iceberg, Flink keeps up — and does it cheaper.
